About Dana's Music Studio

Dana's Music Studio is passionate about helping people love to learn and perform music. Music is an art of expression for the individual creating it and at Dana's Music Studio, lessons are focused around this principle. They goes far beyond just acquiring the usual check boxes of your average piano lesson. Each students individual goals drive their lessons in whichever direction they choose, from wanting to learn a specific song to becoming the next Beyonce, and their goals are free from any judgement or pressure to change.

Students enrolled at the studio also enjoy the benefit of their own private YouTube channels where they can view the latest video updates from their lessons and can literally watch their progress grow and grow.

Dana's Music Studio also hosts Piano Night at Olio's Pizza once a month. This is a free, all ages event, and is a great way to get out and meet with other musicians. Piano Night isn't a recital and you don't have to be the best to play. It's also open to anyone so if you have a friend who plays piano, they're welcome too. If you don't play piano, come anyways and enjoy the delicious food and live music!

Now a little bit about Dana: Dana Pedersen has over 25 years of experience studying piano with numerous teachers and mentors. She has achieved her Royal Conservatory Grade 8 with First Class Honors and her Bachelor of Music, majoring in Performance from the University of Victoria. Nowadays, on top of being a full time piano teacher, she also plays and records with multiple local bands and is very active in the local music scene. She also has a little super power known as perfect pitch and she uses it to its max. With all of these tools in her belt, there’s not a musical challenge she has shied away from. Dana's goal is to get more music out into the local community and she works at this every day. She is passionate about teaching and creating musicians who want to learn and play.

Private Piano Lessons

Private piano lessons for ages 6 - 99. Lessons are customized to suite your goals, from learning a specific song to becoming the next Beyonce. Reading music (notation and chording) as well as ear training, improvisation and theory are essentials taught to all. Students also get their own private YouTube Channel where we track their progress and record their favorites. If you are interested in learning this beautiful and powerful instrument, I would love to be the one to assist you on your musical journey. Contact me today for more information!

Rates: $60/hour (weekly or biweekly) or $40/half-hour (weekly only)
,

Coaching Sessions

Want to learn a song you’ve always loved on piano? Take an old stale piece to the next level? Maybe you want a second opinion? A coaching session is an intense one on one lesson to help you really focus in on a specific song or issue with no weekly commitment required. Online coaching is also available. Contact me for more information and we can get started!

Rates: $60/hour
,

Live Music

Class up your corporate event with some live piano music. Music selection ranges from classical to soft jazz to covers of the classics. Add that something extra to your special event and make it stand out from the rest.

Booking no less than 1 month in advance so contact me today for more information and to reserve your date.

Rates: $100/hour - minimum 1 hour booking
